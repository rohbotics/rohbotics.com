Description:

I have been involved in robotics since I was 8 years old, when I started to build my first Arduino based robots. Since then I have expanded my horizons, learning how to utilize ROS (Robot Operating System) to create more capable robots, as well as learning PCB design to be able to make custom electronics.

I got my Technician class amateur radio license at age 9, and my General class license a year later. I am involved with the ham club FARS and my local ARES/RACES chapter CARES.
